Kapalasastra UGM

Keluarga Pencinta Alam Sastra (Kapalasastra) or commonly abbreviated as Kaps is a Student Association for Environmental and Adventure Activity at the Faculty of Cultural Sciences, UGM. Kapalasastra has four divisions, which are Mountaineering, Rafting, Climbing, and Caving.
